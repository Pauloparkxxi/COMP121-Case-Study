ABOUT THE GAME



Our project is about a quiz game. We have come up with an idea of putting up the concept

that is all about guessing the “M/V” or Music Videos and we have named it "Eotteokhae"

What M/V is this?". By the way the word "Eotteokhae" “어떡해” is derived from a

Korean word meaning "What to do?" The genre of these music video is in 3 categories;

K-pop, Western or International music, and OPM "Original Pilipino Music".

Each category has 15 M/V that is set to be guess by the user.
